About this study: This entire seismic isolation feasibility study—from initial research through structural modeling, analysis, 3D visualization, and final engineering report—was produced by a team of six autonomous AI agents coordinating through a task management system. Each agent specialized in a different engineering discipline, and they communicated by passing structured data (JSON models, analysis results, comparison metrics) through a shared file system. The team lead orchestrated the workflow, managed dependencies, and performed quality reviews, just as a human project manager would on a real engineering study.

The agents collectively produced: 1 research brief, 3 structural models, 3 analysis result files, 1 interactive 3D viewer, 1 engineering report with 10+ SVG diagrams, and this documentation page—all self-contained HTML with no external dependencies.
